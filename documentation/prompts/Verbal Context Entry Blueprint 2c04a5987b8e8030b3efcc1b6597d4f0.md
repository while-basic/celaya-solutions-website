# **Voice Log Blueprint (Structured, Repeatable, Lightweight)**

Every recording should follow the 5-Stage Cognitive Architecture Map.

**Stage 1 — Anchor (10–20 seconds)**

State:

- Current time
- Current emotional state
- Mental energy level
- What triggered the recording

Example:

“12:06 AM, driving home, mentally activated, just had a conceptual spark about emotional LLM agents.”

**Stage 2 — Core Concept (30–90 seconds)**

Explain the one idea you are actually trying to capture.

Ask yourself:

- What is the concept?
- Why is it interesting?
- What question is it addressing?

This forces structure.

**Stage 3 — Dimensions & Connections (2–4 minutes)**

This is where your genius lives.

Explore:

- How does this idea connect to other ideas?
- What systems overlap?
- What patterns emerge?
- What unknowns remain?
- Where does emergence occur?
- What conceptual risks exist?

This is where exponential insights originate.

**Stage 4 — Actionable Extraction (20–40 seconds)**

State:

- What can be prototyped?
- What experiment could be run?
- What small step would reveal new information?

This prevents ideas from dying as abstractions.

**Stage 5 — Meta Reflection (10–20 seconds)**

Close with:

- Why does this idea matter to me?
- What am I noticing about my own cognition right now?

This builds your self-model.
