## Emergency: If You Get Stuck

**On writing:**
- Voice record your thoughts
- Transcribe and light edit
- That's your post

**On perfectionism:**
- Set 25-minute timer
- Write until timer ends
- Publish whatever you have
- Iterate publicly

**On engagement:**
- Reply to 10 people's posts
- Share someone else's work
- Ask a question in a community
- DM someone interesting

**On motivation:**
- Re-read why you started
- Look at your 90-day experiment data
- Remember: this is research documentation
- You're not selling, you're sharing
