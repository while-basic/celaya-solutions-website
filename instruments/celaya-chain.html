Technical Specification
Celaya Chain Protocol.
In detail.
L3
Layer 3
Built on Solana. Layer 3 allows the protocol to inherit Solana's security and finality while implementing the Proof of Coherence consensus mechanism at the application layer.
PoC
Proof of Coherence
The consensus mechanism is not Proof of Work or Proof of Stake. It is Proof of Coherence: validators demonstrate contextual consistency across domains, not computational expenditure.
SAFE
Reputation System
Multi-dimensional reputation scoring: Skill, Accountability, Fidelity, Efficacy. SAFE scores are publicly verifiable and cannot be gamed by a single dimension.
