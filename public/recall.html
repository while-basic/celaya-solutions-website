Why RECALL
Built different.
By design.
01
Internet-free answers
RECALL is hardwired to your documents only. It cannot reach the internet, cannot hallucinate from Wikipedia, and cannot confuse your policies with someone else’s.
02
Every answer has a source
Not just an answer — a citation. File name, page number, exact location. Your team can verify every response in seconds. Auditability built in from day one.
03
Your data stays yours
Deployed on private infrastructure. No third-party training on your documents. Your data exports cleanly any time. Source code in escrow — you are never locked in.

Format-agnostic ingestion
PDFs — scanned or text-based. Word documents. Notion exports. Google Docs. Spreadsheets. SOPs. We handle the OCR and processing. You hand us the files.
